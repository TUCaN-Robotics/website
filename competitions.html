Our Competitions

Eurobot 2026

Eurobot is an international amateur robotics contest open to university students and independent clubs from around the world. Each year, teams design and build autonomous robots to perform specific, themed tasks on a game field, emphasizing high-level programming, sensor integration, and precise mechanical engineering. The competition fosters global collaboration and technical innovation, culminating in a final tournament where the most efficient and creative designs face off in real-time matches.

BattleLab Robotica

BattleLab Robotica is a high-intensity competition focused on heavyweight autonomous sumo wrestling. In this event, custom-built robots must detect and push their opponents out of a circular ring using only internal sensors and pre-programmed logic, with no human intervention allowed during the match. It challenges participants to master the balance between raw power, robust traction, and intelligent defensive maneuvers to dominate the arena.
